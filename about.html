About

At the Pizza Shop, we're proud to bring delicious, family-friendly pizza to central Florida with three convenient locations in North Lakeland, South Lakeland, and Plant City. Whether you're looking to enjoy a meal in our welcoming dining areas or need a quick, tasty dinner to go, we offer a variety of beverages and mouth-watering good that everyone will love.

Our Backstory

Founded with a passion for bringing people together over great food, The Pizza Shop began as a small, local pizzeria in central Florida. Our commitment to quality ingredients and family-friendly service quickly earned us a loyal following, leading to the opening of our locations in North Lakeland, South Lakeland, and Plant City. We continue to delight our community with delicious pizza and a welcoming atmosphere, whether you choose to fine in or take your meal to go.

Our Promise to the Community

At The Pizza Shop, we promise to deliver consistently high-quality, delicious pizza made from the freshest ingredients, while providing a welcoming atmosphere for families to enjoy. Our commitment extends beyond great food; we strive to be an integral part of the central Florida community by supporting local events and offering exceptional service in all three of our locations.

Our Values

At The Pizza Shop, we value:
  • Quality and Freshness – We are dedicated to using only the finest, locally-sourced ingredients to craft our pizzas, ensuring each bite is as fresh and flavorful as possible.
  • Community Engagement – We believe in supporting and enriching our local neighborhoods by participating in community events and fostering a warm, inviting atmosphere in each of our locations.
  • Family Focus – We prioritize creating a family-friendly environment where everyone, from kids to grandparents, can enjoy a delicious meal together and make lasting memories.
